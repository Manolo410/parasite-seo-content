## Stage 1: Research & Scripting (15 min)

Open **[Jasper AI](https://jasper.ai/?ref=dreamsai)** → "Blog Post" or "Video Script" template.

1. Enter your topic (e.g., "how AI voice changers work in 2026")
2. Set tone to "Educational" or "Conversational"
3. Add SEO keywords if targeting search
4. Generate

Jasper produces a structured script with hook, body sections, and CTA — all optimized for retention.

👉 **[Start scripting with Jasper →](https://jasper.ai/?ref=dreamsai)**

## Stage 2: Voiceover (5 min)

Paste your script into **[Murf AI](https://murf.ai/?ref=dreamsai)** .

1. Choose a voice (120+ options, categorized by style)
2. Adjust speed and pitch per sentence if needed
3. Add pauses for emphasis
4. Export as MP3

The voiceover sounds like a professional human narrator. Viewers regularly ask "who's the voice actor?" — it's AI.

👉 **[Try Murf AI Free →](https://murf.ai/?ref=dreamsai)**

---

## Stage 3: Video Creation (20 min)

Import your voiceover into **[Synthesia](https://synthesia.io/?ref=dreamsai)** .

1. Choose an AI avatar (140+ options) or upload your own
2. The avatar automatically lip-syncs to your voiceover
3. Add background, text overlays, and transitions
4. Insert screen recordings for tutorial sections
5. Export in 4K

Total time from script to finished 4K video: under 30 minutes.

👉 **[Create your first AI video →](https://synthesia.io/?ref=dreamsai)**

---

## Stage 4: Thumbnails & Distribution (10 min)

**Thumbnails:** Canva AI generates YouTube thumbnails from your video title — pick the best one and customize.

**Distribution:** Schedule posts across platforms:
- YouTube: Main video
- TikTok/Shorts/Reels: Clip highlights with Opus Clip
- Twitter/X: Thread summarizing key points
- Blog: Full article version (reverse the video script into a blog post)
