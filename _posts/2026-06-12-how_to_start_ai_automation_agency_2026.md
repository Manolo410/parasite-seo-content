## What Is an AI Automation Agency?

You help businesses save time and money by setting up AI workflows:
- Automating customer support with AI chatbots
- Building AI content pipelines for marketing teams
- Setting up AI data analysis and reporting
- Creating AI-powered internal tools

You don't build AI — you implement existing AI tools for businesses that don't have the expertise.

## Step 1: Pick Your Niche

Don't be a "general AI agency." Specialize:

- **E-commerce AI** — Product descriptions, chatbots, email automation
- **Real Estate AI** — Listing descriptions, virtual tours, lead follow-up
- **Content Creator AI** — YouTube automation, blog pipelines, social media
- **Local Business AI** — Review responses, scheduling, customer comms

Pick one. Master it. Then expand.

---

## Step 2: Build 3 Case Studies (Free)

Before charging, do 3 projects for free:
1. Find a business with an obvious AI use case
2. Offer to set it up for free in exchange for a testimonial
3. Document the before/after results (time saved, revenue increased)

These case studies are your portfolio. They're worth more than any certification.

## Step 4: Find Clients

**Fastest methods in 2026:**
1. **Twitter/X** — Post case studies, engage in AI business conversations
2. **LinkedIn** — Direct outreach to business owners mentioning their specific pain point
3. **Upwork** — Bid on "AI automation" jobs (lower pay but fast first clients)
4. **Local businesses** — Walk in or email with a specific AI solution for their industry

A: One client at $500-2,000 setup = $500-2,000. With the right outreach, 2-3 clients in month one is achievable.
