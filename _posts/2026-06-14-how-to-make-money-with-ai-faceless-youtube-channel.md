## 4. The 30-Minute Video Production Workflow

Here's the exact process that turns an idea into a published video in under half an hour:

1. **Script it (5 min):** Paste your topic into Copy.ai's video script template. Edit the hook to be punchier — the first 3 seconds determine whether someone clicks off.
2. **Voice it (3 min):** Copy the script into Speechify. Pick a natural-sounding voice. Export the MP3.
3. **Visualize it (10 min):** Use Synthesia for a talking-head video OR stock footage + text overlays for documentary style. Most faceless channels mix both.
4. **Edit it (10 min):** Add captions (CapCut auto-generates them), trim dead air, insert background music. Aim for snappy pacing — no segment longer than 15 seconds without a visual change.
5. **Package it (2 min):** Write a keyword-rich title, description with your affiliate links, and 5-10 relevant tags. Upload.

That's it. Repeat 5x per week and you've got a content machine.
