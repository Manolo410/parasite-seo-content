## FAQ: AI Voice Changers in 2026

**Q: Can AI voice cloning really copy anyone's voice?**
A: In 2026, yes — with important caveats. Speechify's voice cloning can produce a convincing replica from 30 seconds of clean audio. However, ethical tools require consent verification, and the best clones still need high-quality source audio (no background noise). The technology is incredibly accurate for English and major European languages; less so for tonal languages or strong regional accents.

**Q: Are AI voice changers legal?**
A: The tools are legal. How you use them determines legality. Impersonating someone without consent for fraud, defamation, or harassment is illegal in most jurisdictions. Using voice changers for entertainment, privacy, or creative content is perfectly legal. Always check your local laws regarding consent for voice cloning.
