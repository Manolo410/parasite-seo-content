## Why Standard Rewriters Fail (And What AI Humanizers Do Differently)

Most people try free tools like QuillBot or ChatGPT's built-in rephraser. **They don't work.** Here's why:

Detectors don't just scan for "AI words." They measure:

- **Perplexity** — How predictable your word choices are. AI text is too perfect.
- **Burstiness** — How much your sentence length varies. AI writes unnaturally uniform sentences.
- **Statistical fingerprinting** — The mathematical signature of specific AI models.

Standard rewriters shuffle words but preserve the underlying pattern. AI humanizers fundamentally restructure the text — introducing natural variation, "imperfections," and the unpredictable rhythm of human writing.
