## 2. How Turnitin's AI Detection Actually Works (And Why Most Tools Fail)

Before we rank the tools, you need to understand what you're up against:

- **Layer 1 — Document Classifier:** Scores the entire submission for overall AI probability. If this hits above 15%, the paper is flagged.
- **Layer 2 — Segment Analyzer:** Breaks your paper into overlapping 1,000-word segments and scores each independently. One robotic section can tank the whole submission.
- **Layer 3 — Cross-Reference Layer:** Compares statistical patterns (sentence length variance, vocabulary distribution, transition patterns) against 1.6 billion student submissions.

This is why simple paraphrasing fails. Swapping "utilize" for "use" and rearranging clauses tricks Layer 1 maybe 40% of the time — but Layers 2 and 3 catch the underlying statistical fingerprint. You need a tool that **rebuilds the semantic structure from scratch** while preserving meaning.
