## Pro Tips for Maximum Undetectability

1. **Don't run the same text through twice.** Double-humanizing can introduce unnatural phrasing. One good pass is better than two mediocre ones.
2. **Add your own voice.** After humanization, read through and sprinkle in personal anecdotes, opinions, and stylistic quirks. Detectors look for flat, personality-free prose.
3. **Vary your sources.** Mix humanized paragraphs with genuinely original ones. A 70/30 humanized-to-original ratio fools most detectors.
4. **Use the Aggressive mode sparingly.** It's powerful but can over-paraphrase. Reserve it for content where detection risk is highest.
5. **Pair with a dedicated detector.** StealthWriter's built-in detector is document-level only. Cross-check with **Originality.ai** or **GPTZero** for sentence-level confidence.
